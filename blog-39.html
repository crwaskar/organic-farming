Why should you buy organic food?

The number of shoppers purchase organically raised food but they are concerned about pesticides, additives, antibiotics, and other chemical residues, and they believe organic food is healthier. Concerns about just the impact of conventional farming on biodiversity and the environment, as well as the ethical treatment of animals, are among those factors.
